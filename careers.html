Who We’re Looking For

We are seeking highly qualified individuals with graduate-level expertise in:

  • Botany, Agronomy, or Environmental Biology
  • Aerospace Engineering or Systems Design
  • Astrobiology or Space Agriculture

Preference is given to applicants with prior spaceflight experience or experience in Mars analog environments.

What You’ll Receive

  • Two years of advanced astronaut and agricultural systems training on Earth
  • Permanent residence eligibility in the first Mars colony
  • Opportunity to participate in groundbreaking scientific research
  • Full mission support and innovation funding

How to Apply

Submit your CV, academic credentials, and a personal mission statement through our secure online portal. Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an in-person interview at our Space Training Complex.
