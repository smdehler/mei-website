Mission Overview

The Mars Eden Initiative (MEI) is an international coalition of scientists and engineers dedicated to developing the first sustainable agricultural systems on Mars. Our mission is to enable long-term human habitation through innovation in extraterrestrial horticulture and closed-loop agriculture.

Vision for Mars

MEI envisions thriving greenhouses on Mars where crops are grown using hydroponics, recycled water, and Martian soil simulations. These systems will form the backbone of the first permanent human colony beyond Earth.
